Project Overview

MFU WI-FI This is an innovative project that develops an identity verification system for accessing Wi-Fi at Maefahluang University and the Maefahluang University Medical Center using facial recognition technology through the ThaID application to provide a fast, secure, and seamless connection experience.

Responsibilities: In this project, I am responsible for the design of the user experience (UX/UI) and the development of the web page (Front-end) to enable users to easily start scanning their faces and view the connection status.

Key features

Logging in with face scan

Scan the face through ThaID to connect to wifi.

High security

Verify your identity through ThaID, which is reliable and secure according to government standards.

Duration of wifi usage

After successfully scanning the face, WiFi can be used for 1 hour.
